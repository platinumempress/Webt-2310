Daily Events

Activities

Come out and get into Fun!!

Join the Fun at Our Music Festival! We invite you to dive into the vibrant world of our five-day music festival, where each day is packed with exciting multicultural events and activities! From the Grand Parade and Global Food Fair on opening day to the interactive performance by Butch Locsin and the Festival of Colors on the final day, there's something for everyone. Whether you're eager to learn new dance moves, sample delicious international cuisine, or contribute to a collaborative art piece, our festival offers a unique opportunity to celebrate diversity and creativity. Don't miss out on the nightly concerts, workshops, and spectacular fireworks show that will light up the sky. Come join us and make unforgettable memories with friends and family!

Daily Events

Check out the exciting events happening each day of the festival!

Day 1: Opening Day Extravaganza

The festival kicks off with a Grand Parade featuring vibrant floats and performers from around the world, showcasing traditional costumes and dances. In the afternoon, attendees can enjoy a Global Food Fair, where they can sample delicious dishes from various cultures, from spicy Thai curries to savory Italian pastas. Day 1 wraps up with the opening of the Interactive Balloon Museum, featuring stunning balloon art installations and hands-on activities where visitors can create their own balloon sculptures. The museum will be open throughout the festival, offering a unique and interactive experience for all ages.

Day 2: Rhythm and Beats

Day two is all about rhythm, movement and a little sweetness. The morning starts with a Drumming Workshop led by African percussionists, where participants can learn and play traditional rhythms. In the afternoon, there's a Dance Showcase featuring styles from salsa to Bollywood, inviting everyone to join in and learn some new moves. Day 2 ends with an Amezaiku Workshop, where skilled Japanese artisans demonstrate the intricate art of crafting candy sculptures. Participants can try their hand at creating their own edible masterpieces, learning about the history and techniques of this traditional craft.

Day 3: Art and Expression

Art takes center stage on the third day. The morning begins with a Street Art Tour, highlighting murals and graffiti by local and international artists. In the afternoon, there's an Interactive Art Installation where festival-goers can contribute to a collaborative piece of art. The evening features a Poetry Slam, with poets from different backgrounds sharing their work, celebrating the power of words and storytelling.

Day 4: Cultural Fusion

The fourth day celebrates cultural fusion and collaboration. The day starts with a Cooking Class where chefs from different culinary traditions come together to create fusion dishes. In the afternoon, there's a Fashion Show featuring designers who blend traditional and modern styles from various cultures. The day concludes with a Virtual Reality Games Arena, offering a range of immersive VR experiences that transport participants to different worlds, from exploring ancient civilizations to futuristic adventures.

Day 5: Grand Finale

The final day begins with an Interactive Performance by Mexican performance artist Butch Locsin, known for his vibrant and theatrical style. Following this, the Festival of Colors event invites everyone to throw colored powders in the air, creating a joyful and visually stunning celebration of unity and diversity. The festival concludes with a Phenomenal Fireworks Show, lighting up the night sky in a spectacular display, leaving attendees with unforgettable memories of a week filled with music, culture, and community.

Enjoy 5 days of world class entertainment. You won't want to miss it!!
