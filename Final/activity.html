Activities

Get ready for a fun-filled day at our multicultural music festival! Explore our activity tents and discover new sounds, skills, and friends. Join our Music Workshop and learn to make your own instrument, or show off your skills at our Open Mic Session. Test your knowledge at our Music Trivia Contest, or get moving with our Silent Disco and DJ Sets. Plus, don't miss our Collaborative Performance sessions, where you can jam with fellow music lovers and create unforgettable moments. With something for everyone, our festival is the ultimate celebration of music, diversity, and community!

Open Mic

"Get ready to shine at our Open Mic session! This is your chance to take the stage and share your talents with the world. Whether you're a seasoned performer or just starting out, our Open Mic is the perfect platform to express yourself, showcase your skills, and connect with like-minded music lovers. From spoken word and poetry to music and comedy, we welcome all forms of creative expression. So, come and join the fun, and let your voice be heard!"

Music Trivia

Think you're a music mastermind? Put your knowledge to the test at our Music Trivia Contest! With questions spanning genres, decades, and styles, you'll need to be a true music buff to take home the prize. Gather your friends, form a team, and get ready to rock (or pop, or hip-hop...). Will you be the ultimate music champion?

Collaborative performances

Experience the magic of spontaneous creativity at our Collaborative Performance sessions! Inspired by the art of freestyling, we're bringing together musicians, singers, and performers from diverse backgrounds to create something entirely new and unique. Witness the spark of improvisation as artists come together, feeding off each other's energy and talent to produce unforgettable performances. Who knows what amazing collaborations will be born?"
